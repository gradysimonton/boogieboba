ABOUT US

About Us

My name is Grady S. I have lived in San Diego, California all of my life. My wife and kids love making various smoothies and coffee drinks. A decade ago I was introduced to tapioca balls and milk teas and I was hooked. At the time I wasn't planing on starting a Boba drink shop, but I was urged by the pandemic to do so. Our shop is named after my 2 dogs: Boogie and Bo ~ Boogie Boba. We want to provide the best tasting Boba, teas, and coffee drinks at a great price.
We also want you to get your Boogie ON!
